Hello!

I’m Valentina, Amsterdam-based Front-End Developer & Designer.
Through my work I like to bring the ‘magical realism’ narrative to a meeting point with technology. I am fascinated about creating unusual interactions between ordinary objects and their environment. I use technology to create dialogs between silence and color; sense of calm and motion, or a person’s breathing pace to surfaces of water.
